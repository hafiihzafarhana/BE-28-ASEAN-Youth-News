ASEAN Youth Forum (AYF)

A movement that represents and fights for the younger generation in ASEAN to voice their concerns and strategies to achieve a better ASEAN. AYF has become a platform for youth in ASEAN to voice and demand their rights for a sustainable, inclusive, people-centered, youth-driven regional community. The network aims to institutionalize and establish its national branches to focus engagement on important and timely local issues affecting youth, and consolidate proposals and policy agendas to be submitted to relevant ASEAN offices.
